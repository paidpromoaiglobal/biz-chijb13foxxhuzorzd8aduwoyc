About Us

Valli Makeup artist

Valli Makeup Artist is Puducherry's beloved beauty destination, nestled in the heart of Heritage Town. Led by the talented and passionate Valli akka, the studio has become synonymous with flawless, long-lasting makeup that enhances natural beauty while making every client feel truly special. With a stellar 4.7-star rating and a growing family of happy brides and clients, Valli's artistry speaks for itself.

Specializing in bridal and occasion makeup, Valli brings together skill, precision, and a deep understanding of each client's unique features and personality. Whether it's a grand muhurtham look, an elegant reception appearance, an engagement glow, or a baby shower radiance, every makeover is crafted with love and dedication. Open seven days a week, the studio welcomes you from 10 AM to 8 PM — because your beauty never takes a day off.
